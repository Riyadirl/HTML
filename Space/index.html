All About Planets

There are eight planets in our solar system:

  • Mercury
  • Venus
  • Earth
  • Mars
  • Jupiter
  • Saturn
  • Uranus
  • Neptune

Each planet has its own unique characteristics and features. For example, Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system, while Jupiter is the largest. Earth is the only planet known to support life, while Mars is the focus of ongoing research to determine if it could support life in the future.
